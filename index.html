About

I am a PhD researcher working on knowledge systems, with a focus on unanswerability, uncertainty estimation, and reasoning under incomplete knowledge. My research studies the full QA pipeline, from planning and retrieval to answer generation.

Research Interests

  • Knowledge base Question Answering
  • Unanswerable and ill-posed questions
  • Uncertainty estimation in large language models
  • Self-consistency and Bayesian modeling
  • Closed and open-domain question answering
